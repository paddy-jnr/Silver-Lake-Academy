Our History

Founded in 1995, Silver Lake Academy began with a vision to create an educational institution that would prepare students for a globalized world while maintaining strong roots in local values and community.

What started as a small campus with just 50 students has now grown into a thriving educational community of over 1,200 students from diverse backgrounds and cultures.

Throughout our journey, we've remained committed to our founding principles of academic excellence, character development, and innovation in education.

Message from Our Principal

"Silver Lake Academy, we believe that every child has unique talents and potential waiting to be discovered. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to providing a nurturing environment where students can explore their interests, develop critical thinking skills, and grow into confident, compassionate global citizens."

We combine rigorous academic programs with opportunities for creative expression, physical activity, and community service. This holistic approach ensures that our students are not only prepared for academic success but also for life beyond the classroom.

I invite you to visit our campus and experience the Sunrise difference firsthand. Welcome to our school family!"

Dr. Emily Rodriguez

Principal, Silver Lake Academy
