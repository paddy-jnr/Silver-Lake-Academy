Our Curriculum

At Silver Lake Academy, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that combines the best of international education standards with local cultural values. Our program is designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, and global citizenship.

We follow an inquiry-based learning approach that encourages students to ask questions, explore concepts deeply, and make connections across disciplines. Our curriculum is regularly updated to incorporate the latest educational research and technological advancements.

From early childhood through high school, our academic program prepares students for higher education and lifelong learning with a strong foundation in core subjects and specialized electives.

Grade Levels

Elementary School

Grades 1-5

Foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and social development through hands-on learning experiences.

Middle School

Grades 6-8

Subject-specific instruction with emphasis on critical thinking, research skills, and exploratory electives.

High School

Grades 9-12

College-preparatory curriculum with Advanced Placement courses and specialized pathways in STEM, arts, and humanities.

Academic Departments

Humanities Department

Our Humanities department offers a comprehensive program that explores human culture, society, and historical development. Students develop critical thinking, research, and analytical skills through the study of history, geography, economics, and civics.

Courses Offered:

  • World History
  • U.S. History
  • Human Geography
  • Economics
  • Government & Politics
  • Psychology
  • Sociology

Department Highlights:

  • Model United Nations club
  • Historical research projects
  • Community service initiatives
  • Annual history fair

STEM Department

The STEM department focuses on developing scientific literacy, mathematical reasoning, and technological competence. Our state-of-the-art labs and equipment provide hands-on learning experiences that prepare students for careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Courses Offered:

  • Biology (AP available)
  • Chemistry (AP available)
  • Physics (AP available)
  • Environmental Science
  • Calculus (AP available)
  • Computer Science
  • Engineering Design

Department Highlights:

  • Robotics team competitions
  • Science Olympiad participation
  • STEM research symposium
  • Industry partnership programs

Arts Department

Our Arts department nurtures creativity and self-expression through various media and performing arts. Students develop technical skills, aesthetic appreciation, and the confidence to share their unique perspectives with the world.

Courses Offered:

  • Visual Arts (Drawing, Painting, Sculpture)
  • Digital Media Arts
  • Photography
  • Music Theory & Performance
  • Theater Arts
  • Dance
  • Art History (AP available)

Department Highlights:

  • Annual art exhibition
  • Theater productions
  • Music recitals and concerts
  • Guest artist workshops

World Languages Department

Our World Languages department prepares students for global citizenship through language acquisition and cultural understanding. We offer multiple language options with immersive learning experiences that develop proficiency in speaking, listening, reading, and writing.

Languages Offered:

  • Spanish (all levels, AP available)
  • French (all levels, AP available)
  • Mandarin Chinese (all levels)
  • German (beginner and intermediate)
  • Latin (beginner and intermediate)

Department Highlights:

  • Language immersion trips
  • Cultural celebration events
  • Language certification programs
  • Exchange student programs
